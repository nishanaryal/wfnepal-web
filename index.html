Emergency Food Support during COVID-19

Women Foundation Nepal (WFN) has partnership with many local government for the Emergency Good Support. We primarly focus on collecting and distributing the Food for the families who are facing difficuilty in managing food as of the ongoing nationwide lockdown during COVID. The increasing number of COVID Infections and the ongoing nationwide lockdown here in Nepal has severely affected the women and children to great extent causing the increased mortality and morbidity as they face a greater risk of malnutrition.

The devastating outreach of COVID19 Second wave has caused large numbers of families to lose their family members causing the blockage of the financial crisis. Especially women and children as they are one of the most vulnerable groups during emergencies and disasters.

About Women Foundation Nepal

WFN is a non-profit and non-governmental social development organization established in 2006 by a group of professionals who understood dignity, shared a deep sense of social accountability; continue to embrace a strong desire to address the situation of rural community and women.

WFN focuses on first empowering and finally improving the existing support system of deprived and marginalized community through education, empowerment as well as ensuring entrepreneurship opportunity in Nepal.  

One of the major beliefs of WFN is to increase positive change through mobilizing social awareness, identifying and mobilizing local resources, identifying human capital of country encouraging the participation of youth, women group for economic and social development and self-reliance of the families.
